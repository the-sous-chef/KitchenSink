# V-Model Requirements Specification: Recipe Digitization & Family Circles

**Feature Branch**: `011-recipe-digitization`
**Created**: 2026-05-10
**Status**: Draft
**Source**: `specs/011-recipe-digitization/spec.md`, `specs/011-recipe-digitization/plan.md`, `specs/011-recipe-digitization/pre-impl-review.md`, `specs/011-recipe-digitization/analyze-report.md`

## Overview

Feature 011 delivers a photo-to-recipe digitization pipeline plus Circle-based sharing. This requirements baseline formalizes all feature-level FR/NFR/clarification constraints, plus Phase 13 addendum conditions (T093–T100) required before/alongside implementation. Each requirement is atomic, testable, and backward-traceable.
