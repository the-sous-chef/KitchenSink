### PRF-HAZ-006 — HAZ-033 ALARP Justification Does Not Explicitly State No Further Practicable Reduction Exists

| ------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Severity**       | Minor                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| **Location**       | HAZ-033; §ALARP Acceptance                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| **Description**    | HAZ-033 records Severity=Catastrophic, Likelihood=Improbable, and pre-mitigation Risk Level=Undesirable.
